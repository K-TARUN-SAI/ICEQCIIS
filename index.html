About

About KLEF

Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation (KLEF), also known as KL Deemed to be University, is a premier Category-1 deemed university in Andhra Pradesh, India established in 1980, and widely recognized for its excellence in engineering and technical education. In the NIRF 2025 rankings, KLEF secured the 26th position nationally. The university features a sprawling 100-acre "Green Fields" campus near Vijayawada. The Department of Internet of Things was started in the academic year 2021, with a well-equipped IoT, Embedded Systems Lab and Research and Development wing.

About INTI International University

INTI International University, established in 1986 and located in Nilai, Malaysia, is a premier private university recognized for high employability and international partnerships. It offers diverse programs in business, engineering, IT, and health sciences. Recognized as a 4-star institution in the 2022 QS World University Rankings for Asia. INTI emphasizes industry-relevant, technology-driven education with over 500 corporate partners.

About IoT Department

The Department of Internet of Things (IoT) at KLEF is a forward-looking hub established to drive innovation in next-generation technologies. As the world shifts from traditional internet usage to smart, connected ecosystems. IoT is transforming homes, industries, cities, and transportation. Our program integrates futuristic domains including AI, Cybersecurity, Embedded Systems, Cloud Computing, and Full-Stack IoT Development. Students gain hands-on expertise to design intelligent, secure, and scalable solutions for real-world applications across diverse sectors. With strong industry collaboration, state-of-the-art labs, and project-based learning, the department prepares graduates for high-impact careers. Established in 2021, the IoT Department continues to lead KLEF's mission of shaping future-ready technology professionals.

About the Conference

International Conference on Emerging Quantum Computing and IoT Technologies for Intelligent Security (ICEQCIIS-2026) aims to bring together researchers, academicians, industry experts, and students from across the globe to exchange ideas, present research findings, and discuss emerging challenges and solutions in the areas of Quantum Computing, Internet of Things (IoT), and Intelligent Security Systems. The conference emphasizes multidisciplinary research, integrating engineering, sciences, healthcare, mathematics, and emerging technologies to address real-world security and communication challenges.

Conference Objectives

  • Promote research in quantum-enabled secure communication systems
  • Explore secure and intelligent IoT architecture
  • Address post-quantum cryptography and cyber security challenges
  • Encourage multidisciplinary and collaborative research
  • Foster academia-industry interaction at an international level

Call for Papers & Publication

Submission Guidelines

  • Experimental investigation with specific conclusions
  • Original contribution, not published earlier
  • Plagiarism should be less than 10% (Turnitin)
  • Maximum authors: 4
  • At least one author must register to present

Format: Times New Roman, 12pt, 1.5 spacing. Margins: Left 1.5", others 1.0". Abstract 11pt Italic.

Publication

Accepted papers will be published in conference proceedings. Selected high-quality papers will be invited for extension in:

  • PERTANIKA Journal of Science and Technology (Scopus Q2)
  • International Journal of Advances in Signal and Image Sciences (Scopus Q1)
  • Other Scopus/WoS Journals: AIMS Agriculture, World Journal of Engineering, Millennial Asia, etc.
